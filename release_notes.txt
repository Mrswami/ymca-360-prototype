# Release Notes - YMCA 360 (v1.0.0)

## 🚀 New Features
*   **Smart Scheduling**: Book Personal Training sessions with real-time availability checking. supports 30, 60, and 90-minute slots.
*   **Role-Based Access**: Experience the app as a Member, Trainer, or Manager.
    *   *Trainers*: Manage your weekly availability directly in the app.
    *   *Managers*: View member details and contact info.
*   **Digital ID Card**: Quick-access barcode scanner for gym entry.
*   **Calendar Integration**: Instantly add booked sessions to your Google Calendar.
*   **Notifications**: Get instant confirmations and reminders for upcoming sessions.
*   **On-Demand Workouts**: Browse our library of Yoga, Cardio, and Strength training videos.

## 🛠 Improvements
*   **Enhanced UI**: New visual design with immersive background imagery and a clean, readable interface.
*   **Performance**: Optimized load times for the scheduling engine.
*   **Permissions**: Updated support for Android 13+ Notification permissions.
